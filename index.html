FAQ

Do you discriminate in any way?

No! We love everyone who joins our server equally, and we care for everyone.

What is DPT?                                 

DPT is a community for the disabled, by the disabled. We welcome anyone with any illnesses, diversities, or disabilities; whether that's anxiety, depression, Tourette's Syndrome, missing limbs, ASD... you name it! If you have any physical, mental, emotional, eating, hormonal, or neurological disabilities/diversities/disorders, this is the place for you! Diversity Party Time is a server for support, as well as just hanging out and having fun. There are channels for all your numerous needs. Everyone is welcome!

Is DPT LGBTQ+ friendly?

YES! We accept everyone, no matter their sexuality or gender. We even have multiple LGBTQ+ dedicated channels!
